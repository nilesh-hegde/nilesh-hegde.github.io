Hi!

I am a second year MS in Artificial Intelligence student at Khoury College of Computer Sciences (Northeastern University). I am broadly interested in the applications of machine learning (ML) to the computational sciences, computer vision (CV) and natural language processing (NLP).

My research interest focuses on computational color constancy, computational social science, machine learning optimization and algorithmic graph theory. My goal is to design practical systems as well as understand the underlying human intelligence behind them. Recently I have been exploring the field of bayesian statistics and stochastic processes.

I was fortunate enough to be guided and mentored by so many great reseachers and I deeply appreciate their help. I was part of Prof. Bruce Maxwell's research group at Northeastern while working on Color constancy and Prof. Suresh M Hegde's Graph Theory and Combinatorics group at NIT Surathkal while working on Graph labeling.

I graduated with a B.E. (Hons.) in Computer Science and Engineering from B.M.S. College of Engineering, Bengaluru, India. After graduating, I worked at Unilever as a Data Scientist.
